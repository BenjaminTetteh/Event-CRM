# Event-CRM & Quote Engine

A production business application designed and developed to solve a real operational challenge in event planning: manual quotation generation and fragmented lead management.

Built for a luxury event decor business and currently in active use, the platform streamlines quote generation, lead qualification, and team coordination through a secure real-time CRM and automation workflow.

---

## 🚀 Real-World Impact

This system was developed to address a live business problem.

### Problem

The business relied on manual processes for:

- Preparing client quotations  
- Managing incoming leads  
- Tracking quote status  
- Coordinating staff activity  

This created:

- Slow quote turnaround  
- Pricing inconsistencies  
- Operational inefficiencies  
- Lost time and potential revenue leakage  

---

### Solution

Designed and implemented a custom web application that:

- Automates quotation generation  
- Centralizes lead management  
- Applies budget-based lead qualification  
- Enables real-time collaboration across staff  
- Improves consistency, speed, and operational efficiency  

---

### Result

✅ Deployed in a real business environment  
✅ Currently used in operations  
✅ Proven effective in improving quote generation workflow
